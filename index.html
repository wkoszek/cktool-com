About CK Tool

More Value.
Every Part.

Founded in 1969, CK Tool is a precision machine shop with over 55 years of manufacturing experience. We operate 11 CNC machines with 8 skilled employees in our own 5,300 sq ft facility in Mountain View, CA — owner-occupied for long-term stability.

We love working with local companies — it allows us to interact closely with your engineering team for value-added feedback on saving costs. We also work with companies outside the area and ship product nationwide.
